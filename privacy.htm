  • Account & Profile Data It includes your username and password, Name, email id, purchases or orders made by you, preferences, feedback and survey responses.
  • Contact Data It includes billing address, email address and telephone numbers
  • Transaction Data It includes details about payments to and from you and other details of products you have purchased from us including the type of transaction.
  • Technical Data It includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform and other technology on the devices you use to access this site.

  • To provide the Services and personalize your experience: We use information about you to provide the Services to you, including to process transactions with you, authenticate you when you log in, provide customer support, and operate and maintain the product related services.
